What started as a small fix—using one AI to rewrite prompts for another—became something deeper.

At first, I just wanted cleaner output. So I asked one model to help me write better prompts for another. But as I kept iterating, a structure emerged:

- One model sharpens the question
- Another executes cleanly
- Sometimes a third evaluates
- And the human? Conducts the flow

Not a trick. Not a hack. A system.

**The Arc:**

1. **Help Me Help You (Help Me)** — How I accidentally built a two-model relay to reduce friction
2. **When One AI Rewrites for Another** — The emerging pattern of LLM-to-LLM refinement
3. **Prompt Strategists, Agent Orchestras, and What Comes Next** — Why prompt refinement is just the first stage
4. **How I Work With AIs (And Why)** — A look inside the real workflow
5. **From Prompt to Pattern** — Wrapping the arc and opening a new question

Each post explores a layer: intention, structure, delegation, synthesis. Together, they point toward something larger—we're not just prompting tools, we're learning to think *with* them.
