## The Trust Problem

<div className="bg-rose-500/10 border border-rose-500/20 rounded-xl p-6 mb-8">
<div className="text-white/60 text-sm uppercase tracking-wider mb-4">The most significant risk isn't economic—it's epistemic.</div>

<p className="text-xl text-white/90 leading-relaxed mb-6">

</p>

<Callout color="rose">

</Callout>
</Slide>
