## The Determinism Fallacy

<p className="text-xl text-white/60 mb-10">Why the flight simulator analogy breaks:</p>

<div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-8">
  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="text-emerald-400">●</span> Aviation (Deterministic)
    </h3>
    <p className="text-white/70 mb-4">Governed by physical laws. Correct inputs produce predictable outputs.</p>
    <div className="bg-black/40 rounded-lg p-4 text-white/50 text-sm">
      If the pilot executes correctly, the plane recovers. Physics doesn't have bad days.
    </div>
  </Card>

  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="text-rose-400">●</span> Business (Stochastic)
    </h3>
    <p className="text-white/70 mb-4">Driven by human psychology, politics, irrationality. Correct inputs may produce unpredictable outputs.</p>
    <div className="bg-black/40 rounded-lg p-4 text-white/50 text-sm">
      The real CFO rejects good logic for reasons they won't articulate and wouldn't admit.
    </div>
  </Card>
</div>

<Callout color="rose">
**Synthetic users regress to the mean.** They're too rational, too polite, too willing to agree with good logic. Real clients are irrational, political, and emotionally driven.
</Callout>
</Slide>

## The Shadow Subsidy

<p className="text-xl text-white/60 mb-10">Reinvest AI efficiency gains into human mentorship:</p>

<div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-8">
  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4">The Mechanism</h3>
    <p className="text-white/70 mb-4">For every AI-augmented project, assign a Shadow Junior. Their role:</p>
    <ul className="list-none space-y-2 text-white/60 text-sm">
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">✗</span>
        Not to produce deliverables (AI does that)
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">✓</span>
        To sit in the room and observe
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">✓</span>
        To take notes on social dynamics
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">✓</span>
        To debrief with the partner afterward
      </li>
    </ul>
  </Card>

  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4">The Debrief Questions</h3>
    <div className="space-y-4 text-white/70 text-sm">
      <p className="italic border-l-2 border-cyan-400 pl-4">"What did you notice when the CFO's voice changed?"</p>
      <p className="italic border-l-2 border-cyan-400 pl-4">"Why do you think the CEO didn't push back on the timeline?"</p>
      <p className="italic border-l-2 border-cyan-400 pl-4">"When I paused before answering, what signal was I reading?"</p>
    </div>
  </Card>
</div>

<div className="bg-emerald-500/10 border border-emerald-500/20 rounded-xl p-6">
**Shadow Time is non-billable but explicitly funded.** AI efficiency creates margin headroom. Reallocate a portion to tacit knowledge transfer.
</div>
