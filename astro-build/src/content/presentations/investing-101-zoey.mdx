## Why Rebalancing Works

<p className="text-xl text-white/60 mb-10">It's a mechanical "buy low, sell high" system that removes emotion and guesswork.</p>

<div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-8">
  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="text-emerald-400">●</span> What You're Actually Doing
    </h3>
    <ul className="list-none space-y-3 text-white/70">
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Selling winners</span> — taking profits from what went UP</span>
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Buying losers</span> — buying more of what went DOWN (it's on sale)</span>
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-emerald-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Automatically</span> — no guessing, no emotion, just math</span>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </Card>

  <Card>
    <h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="text-rose-400">●</span> What Most People Do Instead
    </h3>
    <ul className="list-none space-y-3 text-white/70">
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-rose-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Buy winners</span> — chase what's already gone up (buying high)</span>
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-rose-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Sell losers</span> — panic sell what dropped (selling low)</span>
      </li>
      <li className="flex items-start gap-2">
        <span className="text-rose-400 mt-1">→</span>
        <span><span className="text-white">Emotionally</span> — reacting to news, fear, excitement</span>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
</div>

<div className="bg-gradient-to-br from-indigo-500/20 to-cyan-500/10 border border-indigo-500/30 rounded-2xl p-6">
<h3 className="font-bold text-lg mb-3">The Key Insight</h3>
<p className="text-white/80 mb-0">
Rebalancing <span className="text-cyan-400 font-semibold">forces you to do the opposite of your instincts</span>. When stocks crash and everyone is scared, rebalancing tells you to buy more. When stocks soar and everyone is greedy, rebalancing tells you to take profits. Over decades, this mechanical discipline beats emotional trading almost every time.
</p>
</div>
