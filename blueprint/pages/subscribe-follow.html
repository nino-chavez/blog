Follow the publication

Follow on RSS, not email.

Two RSS feeds plus a LinkedIn link. No email field, no signup flow, no welcome series. Reader sovereignty is a positioning signal — handled below.

⚠ Why no email signup

An email list converts readers into addressable inventory. Publications that chase email start to optimize for capture rate, which warps the writing.

Most readers of a serious technical publication arrive via search or referral, follow what they value, and skip what they don't. RSS makes that frictionless. Email makes it transactional.

If you don't already use an RSS reader

Three solid choices: NetNewsWire (free, Apple-native), Feedly (web + mobile), Inoreader (heavy-power features). All three handle full-content feeds correctly. Add the feed URL above and you're done.

…or follow on LinkedIn for thread-level discussion

Dispatches are cross-posted as LinkedIn essays with the same body, no excerpting. Threads under those posts are where the longer arguments play out — counterpoints land there before they make it back into a dispatch.
