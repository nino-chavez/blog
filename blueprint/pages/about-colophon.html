Welcome to [v2-name]. The publication is a working journal of agent-assisted production work — the chassis that lets a small team operate at director-tier output, the contracts that catch what humans miss, the receipts that make the work legible to a peer architect or a hiring evaluator looking for proof.

The premise: most discussion of AI in software is either credulous (this changes everything) or dismissive (this changes nothing). Neither matches what's happening on the ground. This publication tracks what changed — and what didn't — using receipts, not arguments from authority.

Posts are written through agents, then human-reviewed and human-published. The voice belongs to Nino; the cadence and rigor compound through the same chassis the dispatches describe. That's the point.

Methodology

The stack behind the dispatches

Every surface of this publication was composed deterministically through a forge pipeline. The work isn't decoration — it's the substrate that makes director-tier output legible at a team of one.

forge-brand Brand kit → CSS tokens, voice rules, image-gen style — generated from research, not picked from a designer-tool default.
forge-signal Voice-scored copy generation. Every string passes against the 5 attributes; failures regenerate instead of shipping.
gen-images Per-surface editorial illustration through AI image models, prompted from the brand kit's style.json.
forge-site Publication archetype — composition playbook. Defines the surface set, the anti-modules (no email capture), and the editorial rules.
blueprint Seven-stage pipeline that produced this surface set. Brownfield audit → research → design principles → prescription → prototype → deploy.
