What this publication is

A working architect's notes on AI-era engineering, agentic systems, governance, and the practice of the craft. Long-form essays as the flagship surface; companion whitepapers when an idea earns the technical treatment; counterpoints when a position needs stress-testing; series when an argument runs across multiple pieces.

It is a publication, not a personal-brand newsletter. There is no email capture, no subscriber-count display, no "Now reading" widget. Follow via RSS or LinkedIn if it's earned the slot in your reading life.

Cadence

Published when ready. No promised cadence. The publication ships when a piece earns its place; preserves the no-marketing-tells posture and aligns with the hiring-evaluator persona's drop-reasons (per D4, accepted under Thesis A).

Format conventions
PostsLong-form essays in MDX. Reading column capped at 720px. Callouts and pullquotes used sparingly. Show-the-work voice.
WhitepapersFormal companions to specific posts. Plain markdown. Table-heavy. Executive Summary self-contained. companionOf binding to parent.
SeriesNumbered ordered sequences. Reads as one argument across multiple parts. Read-state tracked client-side (localStorage; no server tracking).
CounterpointsSelf-red-team or external-critic responses to specific posts. challengesPost binding renders auto-link banner on the parent. Reader-facing label: Counterpoint (per D8 — methodology terms as the reader-facing vocabulary, same as Stratechery / lethain / charity.wtf).
VoiceInherits the v1 voice guide unchanged. No delta (per D5, accepted under Thesis A — the 156-post corpus voice IS the asset; consistency is the load-bearing affordance for the hiring-evaluator persona's cross-post sampling).
Colophon

Built on Astro 5 with MDX. Deployed via Cloudflare Pages. Tags canonicalized in astro-build/src/config/tags.ts. Sitemap and RSS regenerated per build. No analytics tracker; no third-party cookies. Source: github.com/nino-chavez/blog.
