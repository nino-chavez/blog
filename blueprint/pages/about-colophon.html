What this publication is

A working architect's notes on AI-era engineering, agentic systems, governance, and the practice of the craft. Long-form essays as the flagship surface; companion whitepapers when an idea earns the technical treatment; counterpoints when a position needs stress-testing; series when an argument runs across multiple pieces.

It is a publication, not a personal-brand newsletter. There is no email capture, no subscriber-count display, no "Now reading" widget. Follow via RSS or LinkedIn if it has earned the slot in your reading life.

Author

Nino Chavez — Architect who directs agents, not an engineer who types code. Director-tier work, executed through a team budget of zero. Peer group: architects, principal consultants, fractional CTOs, directors of AI engineering.
